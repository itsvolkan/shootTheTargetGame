Shoot the Target Game

How to Play

  • Objective: The objective of the game is to shoot down as many balloons as possible to earn points. The game will get progressively harder as you advance through the levels.
  • Starting the Game: Click on the "Start" button to begin the game.
  • Gameplay: Balloons will start appearing from the top of the screen. Click on them to shoot them down. Each shot balloon earns you 10 points.
  • Scoring: Your current score will be displayed on the top-right corner of the game area. The "Score" will increase every time you successfully shoot a balloon.
  • Levels: As you earn more points, you will progress through different levels. Each level has a different target score to reach.
  • Level Up: When your score reaches the target score for the current level, you will level up. The game will become faster and more challenging with each level.
  • Exiting the Game: Click the "Exit" button to close the game.
  • Restarting the Game: press the "F5" key on your keyboard to reset the game if needed.
